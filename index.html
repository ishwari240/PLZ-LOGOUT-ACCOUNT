Why Logging Out Matters

Using public computers in cyber caf√©s, college libraries, or labs is common. But forgetting to log out of accounts like Google, GitHub, or social media can expose your personal and professional data.

Importance of Logging Out

Logging out ensures that your personal data remains private and protected. Public computers can be accessed by many users, making them unsafe for leaving accounts logged in.

  • Protects personal emails and files
  • Prevents misuse of GitHub repositories
  • Stops unauthorized access to cloud services

Risks of Not Logging Out

If you forget to log out, the next user can:

  • Read or send emails from your account
  • Change your passwords
  • Delete or modify GitHub projects
  • Access saved passwords and browsing history

Safety Tips

  1. Always log out after use
  2. Use private/incognito mode
  3. Avoid saving passwords on public PCs
  4. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  5. Clear browser data before leaving
